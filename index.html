About Us

Google Developer Groups (GDG) are for developers who are interested in Google's developer technology. We aim to create a collaborative environment where developers can learn, share, and grow together.

Our Mission

To empower developers and tech enthusiasts by providing a platform to learn, connect, and innovate using Google's cutting-edge technologies.

What We Do

We organize workshops, hackathons, and meetups to help developers enhance their skills and stay updated with the latest trends in technology.
